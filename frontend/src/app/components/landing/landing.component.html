We believe graffiti is the last real art. Undomesticated, wild and full of potential. BLCKBOOK is as much of a loveletter to the community of streetart and graffiti as it is a game. There is an unlimited amount of creativity around you. All you need to is to open your eyes

BLCKBOOK is a community-based platform. The community decides on which Graffiti gets minted by voting every week.

HOW IT WORKS

To succeed in BLCKBOOK, all it takes is for you to see amazing Graffiti and take a photo of it. Every week, the best Graffiti get auctioned and the finders get to keep the lion’s share. No time to be outside? No problem, just vote on the coolest graffiti and earn crypto that way.
