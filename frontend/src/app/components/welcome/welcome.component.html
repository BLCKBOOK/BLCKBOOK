FAQ IS BLCKBOOK LEGAL?

Yes. We will only launch in legislations where minting Graffiti is legal in terms of copyright law. In other legislations, we intend to cooperate directly with accredited street artists. Painting graffiti yourself is illegal however, and we strongly advise against it.

 HOW IS IT DETERMINED WHICH GRAFFITI GETS MINTED?

Every week, all players can upload one graffiti of which they take a photo. After the deadline, all players get to vote on 3 graffitis which they like themselves. The top 10% of graffiti with the most votes then get auctioned.

WHO MAKES MONEY WITH BLCKBOOK AND HOW?

The person that uploads the graffiti gets 60% of earnings generated by the auction. 15% of earnings get distributed to all people who voted for the graffiti, 15% of earnings get distributed to the gang that controls the area the graffiti is located in. The remainder gets distributed to BLCKBOOK. Rates may shift over time.

 HOW DOES BLCKBOOK RUN ON THE BLOCKCHAIN?

BLCKBOOK runs on Tezos and all NFTs are based on the FA2 standard. Subsequently, all earnings get paid out in TEZ.

HOW DO YOU PREVENT DUPLICATES?

Once a graffiti is minted, the same graffiti can never be minted via BLCKBOOK ever again. We use a combination of Machine learning and manual review to prevent duplicates. During the same voting period, the person who photographs the graffiti first counts.
